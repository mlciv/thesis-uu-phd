\chapter{Structures in Natural Language}
In the following two sections, first, we will briefly review the
background on symbolic representation for natural language including
broad-coverage meaning representations and application-specific
representations. Then, we will give an overview of the linguistic
structure prediction and recent advances on deep structured prediction.
